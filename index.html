Map Percentage
The % on each ward shows that ward's share of the selected resource type out of the total citywide count — e.g. 4% means that ward holds 4% of all parks in Toronto.
Income Class
Wards are classified as Low, Medium, or High income based on the 33rd and 67th percentile of median household income across all 25 wards.
Resource Gap
The zero baseline is the median resource count across all 25 wards — representing the typical Toronto ward.
Bar Direction
Bars extending right indicate above-median access. Bars extending left indicate fewer resources than the typical Toronto ward.
